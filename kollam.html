KOLLAM

Jatayu Earth's Centre

The top sightseeing places in Kollam are Thevalli Palace, Amritapuri, Mahatma Gandhi Beach and Park, Jatayu Earth's Centre, Punalur, Palaruvi Waterfalls.

Rich in history

Kollam is a city in the state of Kerala, on India's Malabar Coast. It’s known as a trade hub and for its beaches, like lively Kollam and secluded Thirumullavaram.
