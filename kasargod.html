KASARGOD

Land of Gods

The northernmost district of Kerala, Kasaragod is famous for its handlooms and its 293 kilometre long coastline. With its cultural uniqueness, the place is known as the land of gods.

Rich in history

Kasaragod is home to the largest and best preserved fort in the State - Bekal. The place is also popular for its ancient temples and rich biodiversity. It is an important border district as well that regulates trade with a lot of neighbouring States.
