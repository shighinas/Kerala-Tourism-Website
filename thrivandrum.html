THIRUVANANDAPURAM

Padmanabhapuram Palace

Thiruvananthapuram (or Trivandrum) is the capital of the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's distinguished by its British colonial architecture and many art galleries.

Lots to see

Major tourist attractions and sightseeing places to visit in Trivandrum - Padmanabha Swamy Temple, Agasthyakoodam, Padmanabhapuram Palace and many more.
