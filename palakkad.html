KASARGOD

Natural Beauty

Ottapalam, Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary, Vadakkanthara Temple, Palakkad Fort, Kanjirapuzha, Dhoni Waterfalls, Malampuzha Garden, Malampuzha Dam, Meenvallam Waterfalls, Jain Temple, Thiruvalathur Randu Moorthy Temple, Rock Garden, and many more.

Rich in history

Palakkad is home to the largest and best preserved fort in the State - Bekal. The place is also popular for its ancient temples and rich biodiversity. It is an important border district as well that regulates trade with a lot of neighbouring States.
