KOTTAYAM

Land of Gods

The northernmost district of Kerala, Kottayam is famous for its handlooms and its 293 kilometre long coastline. With its cultural uniqueness, the place is known as the land of gods.

Rich in history

Kottayam, Changanassery, Pala, Erattupetta, Ettumanoor, and Vaikom. It is the only district in Kerala that does not border the Arabian Sea or any other states. The district is bordered by hills in the east, and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west.
